Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is one of the most famous italian dishes that is very popular amongst any family! It's a go-to meal for families with a taste for comfort food or even a home cooked meal! This dish is to die for and will have the kiddos wanting more!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 Can Tomato Paste
  • 2 Cans Tomato Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder

Directions

  1. Cook ground meat throrougly in skillet
  2. Boil water for pasta while ground meat is cooking
  3. Once water comes to a boil, insert the pasta and cook until done
  4. Drain the ground meat
  5. Pour the drained meat into a pot
  6. Pour the tomato paste and tomato sauce into the pot
  7. Add sugar to sweeten sauces to liking
  8. Add salt, pepper, garlic, and onion as desired
  9. Add Worcestershire sauces as desired
  10. Drain pasta and set aside
