Cheeseburger

Description

Just your ordinary cheeseburger! You can't go wrong with it! If you're looking for comfort food or just something quick and simple, then look no further! Made with a juicy 1/2 pound angus beef patty and topped with your choice of cheese, lettuce, tomatoes etc.

Ingredients

  • 2 Lbs 80/20 Ground Beef Chuck
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder

Directions

  1. Preheat skillet on medium-high heat
  2. Add olive oil to skillet
  3. Combine ground beef into a bowl
  4. Season the mixture as desired
  5. Grab medium sized portions and roll into a ball
  6. Flatten the ball with both hands into a hamburger patty shape
  7. Place patty in warm skillet and cook until done
  8. When almost done, add cheese ontop of patty while in skillet
  9. When cheese is melted, remove patty
